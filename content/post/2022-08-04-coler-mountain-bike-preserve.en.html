Coler Mountain

Coler Moutain offers outstanding trails and even has a walk-in coffee shop, called (https://airshipcoffee.com/)Airship Coffee, that also serves food. I’m not sure if they have wi-fi or not, but it’s definitely an awesome spot. Here is a (https://g.page/Airship-Coler?share)[Google link] for those who want to check it out. Coler Mountain is a must-do, whether you are a local or a visitor.

Airship Coffee

Before I get into the camping, let’s talk coffee. The Airship Coffee shop is the perfect destination cafe. Nothing like having a solid espresso then hitting the trails. I wish we had one of these where I live, because I’d probably go every day! This little shop is nestled right in with the trail system that is basically connected to the campgrounds.

Camping

My camping experience at (https://www.peelcompton.org/coler-mountain-bike-preserve/camping/)[Coler Campground] was great. They have updated, large, clean and air conditioned bathrooms. This was essential after a long hot ride in late July summer heat. The showers have hot water.The site has little trollys to haul your gear to the tent platform and campfire wood for sale. There is a community fire pit but I did not get to use it or see anyone else utilize it. A solid choice for any family.

Location

I cannot say enough good things about this place. I never thought I would like Arkansas but this place inspired me to think about moving to NW Arkansas. What other city has world class trails as an amenity for downtown? This is truly a place for anyone who enjoys mountain biking and the outdoors. There is something here for all skill levels, from easy and flat trails or big drops.

You will also find many stops with bike repair tools and pumps near the preserve. Cease & Desist was closed when I arrived but both Rock Soft and Rock Solid were… well, solid.
