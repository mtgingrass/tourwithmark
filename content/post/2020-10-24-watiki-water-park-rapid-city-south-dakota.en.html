WaTikie Water Park

For the Rapid City area (or lets face it, all of South Dakota and part of Nebraska too), this water park is super fun. There are lots to do for its compact size, but can get very busy. There is a cool bar that has one of the transparent slides go through it. So, every once in a while you see your kiddo fly by in the tube while you are sipping on a delicious IPA inside. They also have a restaurant, arcades, and a few other attractions all in the same area.

As usual, I used my GoPro to film a quick tour. See the video, below.

The staff does an excellent job but watch the younger kids because it does get packed. They allow anyone to use the water park even if you are not staying at the connecting hotels. Weekdays are less busy but sometimes have limited hours. The tables fill up so quickly so grab on when you can. I usually just find a spot along the wall to put my stuff down (hiding valuables in my shoe).

There are slides, a lazy river, a larger obstacle course pool that seems to always be heated more than the others, a hot tub, and a little kid area to choose from. Also, there are tonnes of floats to just lay on and float. The lazy river is only about three feet deep so that is slightly disappointing. However, the kids seem to love it. The air temperature is always great, even on the coldest days. It’s nice knowing that it’s snowing and fridget outside while we are all inside basking in the glory of the heated pools!
