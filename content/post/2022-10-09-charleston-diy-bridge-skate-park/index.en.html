Location & Parking

Use Google Maps or Apple Maps and look for “Palmetto Brewing Company.” Once you find it, you will see a bridge adjacent to the brewing company. Park right under the bridge, and you are golding.

I marked the two areas you can park in the diagram below. The lower P is easier to park at because there are not a lot of divots and holes in the ground. However, you must walk across the street and go to the other parking area to find the DIY Bridge Spot. The lower P has plenty of parking but drive very slow due to the potholes and unpaved sections. Both parking areas are safe, and law enforcement won’t’ mess with you.
